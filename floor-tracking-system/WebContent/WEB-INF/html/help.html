Frequently asked questions
How to configure a new Floor?
Click on the "Configure New Floor"" link in the Choose Floor page.
What the different items on the "Configure New Floor" page signify?
1. ODC Name - Give the name of the ODC(e.g - ODC6)
2. Establishment Name - Give the name of the DC.(e.g - Kalinga Park, Siruseri)
3. City - Give the name of the City.(e.g - Bhubaneswar)
4. Rows - Give the number of logical rows in which you divide the floor
5. Columns - Give the number of logical columns in which you divide the floor
5. ODC Id - Give a unique id for this floor. typically use abbreviated without spaces. e.g. For Kalinga Park, ODC6; use KP_ODC6
How to allocate seats in Configure Floor Page?
If there is a cube enter the cube id in the text box provided in Configure page after you provide the floor details. If there are no cubes or path leave the cube empty.You can click on the preview button to see how the arrange ment looks like
What does the check box in "Configure New Floor" page signify?
There are scenarios where there are some special cubes allocated. These cubes are typically reserved for Printers, Servers etc. If the cube is a special cube (like printer, server etc,) give the name of the cube and check the check box to its right hand side. If the cube Id is not given, then clicking the check box has no effect. You can click on the preview button to see how the arrangement looks like
How can I allocate employees to a specific cube?
Click on the cube. The search results window shall open with a button 'Allocate New'. Click on the button and provide the necessary details for the new employee
How can I update/remove an employee information who is allocated to a cube?
You can search for the employee in the search pane or click on the cube allocated to him. The search results window will open with the 'Update' button. Click on update and provide necessary details. For removing/deallocating the employee click on 'Deallocate' button.
What does 'Add Floater' button signify?
In many situations there are employees who are not allocated to any cubicle. In that case they sit in conference rooms. These employees are called Floater employee. To add a floater employee click on the 'Add Floater' button.
How Can I get a list of all the floater employees ?
In the search window, in the Cube id text box provide "Floater" and click search.
